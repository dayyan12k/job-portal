Our Journey

From a simple idea to a platform that's changing lives

2020 - The Beginning

Founded with a vision to make job searching more human and efficient. Launched our MVP with basic job matching features.

2022 - AI Integration

Introduced AI-powered matching algorithms and personalized job recommendations. Reached 10,000 active users milestone.

2024 - Expansion

Expanded to multiple industries and launched our enterprise solutions. Now serving over 50,000 job seekers and 5,000 companies.

2025 - The Future

Continuing to innovate with new features, global expansion, and our commitment to connecting talent with opportunity worldwide.
